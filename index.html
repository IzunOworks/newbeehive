NewbeeHive

NewbeeHive is an experimental declaration
designed to make it easier for beginners to ask questions.

It is not a service, a forum, or a social network.
Instead, it proposes an attitude for beginner-to-beginner communication ?
how to ask questions, and how to interact ?
using platforms that already exist.

What is NewbeeHive?

NewbeeHive is a way of thinking that allows beginners to ask questions
without fear of being ignored, nitpicked, or told
“this has already been asked many times.”

Rather than creating a new Q&A site or a beginner-only SNS,
NewbeeHive places a declaration that helps people use existing services
in a more relaxed and forgiving way.

The goal is not to get perfect answers.
The goal is to lower the barrier to asking questions.

Declaration: About beginner questions

For beginners, every question is asked for the first time.

The same questions will appear again and again.
This is not laziness ? it is natural.

NewbeeHive values
the person who is struggling right now,
more than archived knowledge or past discussions.

Learning by teaching

In NewbeeHive,
answering questions is also a form of learning.

  • Beginners are welcome to answer beginners
  • Sharing what you found through your own research is enough
  • Answers do not need to be perfect or complete

Asking, researching, and explaining ?
all of these are part of learning.

Teaching how to ask a question is also a way of helping.

About incomplete questions

For beginners,
not knowing what information to include is normal.

If a question is missing details,
asking back ? such as
“Which programming language?” or
“What environment are you using?” ?
is also a valid and helpful interaction.

NewbeeHive assumes that
questions will often be incomplete.

What NewbeeHive does NOT aim to be

NewbeeHive does not aim to be:

  • A permanent knowledge base
  • A managed or moderated community
  • A system for ranking or evaluating questions and answers
  • A place that judges correctness

For this reason,
there are no administrators, moderators, or penalties.

How to use NewbeeHive

NewbeeHive does not provide a place to ask questions.

You can use it anywhere people can read your words ?
X (Twitter), forums, blogs, GitHub Issues, and more.

When asking or sharing,
using the tag #NewbeeHive (or the name itself) is encouraged,
but never required.

Status

NewbeeHive is an ongoing experiment.

It may change.
It may never spread.
You do not need permission to use it.

If it helps you, use it.
If not, feel free to ignore it.
